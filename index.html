Track List

Paste one Spotify track link per line. You can paste dozens or even hundreds of links at once. Spotify URIs and bare 22-character track IDs are also accepted. Duplicate links are removed. When you add links later, existing cards and their categories are preserved; new tracks are distributed across category deficits to keep the totals as even as possible. No track list or metadata is stored after the page is closed.
